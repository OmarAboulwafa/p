Customer Churn prediction

Executive Summary

This report addresses the critical issue of high customer churn (26%) impacting the profitability.
And using machine learning (ML), we developed a predictive model to identify at-risk customers and uncover key drivers of churn. Key findings include the significance of contract type, monthly charges, and customer tenure as primary churn predictors. Strategic recommendations focus on targeted retention campaigns, personalized incentives, and operational improvements to reduce churn and enhance profitability.

Objective

Leverage customer data to build an ML model predicting churn.
Identify actionable factors influencing churn to guide retention strategies.

Hypothesis

Churn is driven by service dissatisfaction, pricing, contract flexibility, and demographic factors.

Background

The telecom industry faces intense competition, making customer retention critical.
A 26% churn rate indicates systemic issues requiring data-driven intervention.
