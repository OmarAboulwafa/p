employees network Analysis

Business Case

Head of HR wants help mapping out the company's employee network using message data. They plan to use the network map to understand interdepartmental dynamics better and explore how the company shares information. The ultimate goal of this project is to think of ways to improve collaboration throughout the company.

Business Demand

  • Which departments are the most/least active?
  • Which employee has the most connections?.
  • Identify the most influential departments and employees.
  • Using the network analysis, in which departments would you recommend the HR team focus to boost collaboration?.
